Know the Difference

There are three main screen failures that I see often. These are Unresponsive screens where no matter what you try to click, nothing happens. There are some that will have blotches or lines that look like the pixels have given out. This is often due to damage of the LCD layer in the phone screen. Or the screen is simply shattered and the glass overlay needs replacing.
